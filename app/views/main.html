Welcome to TandemNet

TandemNet allow you to set your own practice times, no matter the time of day or night, any where you are in the world, to practice speaking and understanding a foreign language through listening and speaking. Connect to native speakers and meet new people through asyncronous audio communications. Pick a partner (or several), or just practice with the community at large.
